# Bajigu Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-08_

Bajigu is built to protect your attention, and that includes your privacy.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Bajigu does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any data.

## What is stored, and where

Bajigu stores a small amount of data **locally on your device only**, using the
browser's local extension storage:

- Your toggle settings (which sites are enabled, whether the new-tab dashboard is on).
- Local counters: how many Shorts/Reels were removed from your feed, how many you
  opened and were redirected, and your day streak.

This data never leaves your device. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics,
and no third-party services.

## Permissions

- **`storage`** — to save the settings and counters described above, locally.
- **`declarativeNetRequest`** — to redirect Shorts URLs to the normal video player.
- **Host access to `youtube.com` and `facebook.com`** — so the extension can hide
  Shorts/Reels and redirect on those sites. Bajigu does not read, collect, or send
  the content of those pages anywhere.

## What we never do

- No tracking or analytics.
- No ads or affiliate links.
- No changing your default search engine.
- No remote code execution.
