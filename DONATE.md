- **It's the only fuel there is.** No ad revenue, no VC runway, no premium upsell.
  Every contribution is the entire budget.
- **YouTube and Facebook fight back constantly.** They reshuffle their HTML every few
  weeks, and each time, a rule can break and Shorts leak back in. Your support buys
  the **maintenance time** that keeps Bajigu working instead of quietly rotting.
- **You're not tipping a company.** You're backing one parent who decided to build a
  tool that *gives* time back instead of an app designed to *steal* it.
- **If Bajigu handed you back even one evening** — for your kids, your partner, your
  sleep, your own quiet — that's already worth a coffee.

> The whole point of Bajigu is that your attention is too valuable to give away for
